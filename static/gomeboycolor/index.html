About

This is gomeboycolor, a Gameboy Color emulator, written in Go, ported to WebAssembly.

The performance is not great, and it's not a perfect emulator, but most games should work. I coded this a few years ago and decided to try and port it to WebAssembly.

⚠️ Google Chrome users ⚠️

Performance, at least for this application, is very sketchy in Chrome in comparison to Firefox and Safari.

You might find keyboard controls and the 'Stop' button become unresponsive in Chrome, so by default I've locked the frame rate to 25 fps.

Features
  • ✅ ROMs that support battery saves are stored in your browser LocalStorage.
  • ❌ Audio is not implemented
  • ✅ Gameboy Color support
